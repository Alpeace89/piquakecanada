In the last 24 hours, we've had two small tremors with a magnitude of M1.65 each, both happening near Coaticook in Quebec. The latest one happened just after noon on April 2nd and was at a place called Dynamitage or Blast, about 8 km south of Coaticook. It's the first time we've seen two small tremors reported from this area in less than 24 hours.
