In the last 24 hours, we had 10 reports of quakes in Canada. The largest one was a M4.31 near Tuktoyaktuk that happened on April 26th at around 11 PM. In the latest event, which occurred this afternoon, it was a smaller quake measuring M1.59 and hit Whitehorse area. Most of these quakes were in Yukon Territory with one each from Nunavut and Alaska.
