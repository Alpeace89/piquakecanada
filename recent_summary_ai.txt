The PiQuake Canada dashboard is tracking nine events in the last 24 hours. The largest one was a M4.15 quake, which happened just west of Burwash Landing in the Yukon and a bit east of Hinton in Alberta at around 1 p.m. yesterday. And earlier today, we had a M3.16 shake near Hinton that was likely from dynamite blasting. These happen all over Canada with most activity in the Yukon and Alberta.
